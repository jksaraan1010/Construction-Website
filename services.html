Services At Saraan Roofing & Repair
  1. Ice and Water Shields:
    We apply a special modified bitumen product at critical areas, eaves and valleys.
    Ice and water shield does just what it is name implies; it helps prevent leaks
    and guards against ice damming.


  2. Roof-Deck Protection:
    We apply a 15 pound ASTM felt paper over the entire roof to protect against
    wind-driven rain and to protect the shingles from chemicals in the roof deck boards.


  3. Quality Shingles:
    We offer time-tested roof shingles made by Owens Corning, GAF, and CertainTeed.
    These shingles have proven to be the top performers providing superior appearance
    and durability.


  4. Attic Ventilation:
    We ensure the attic is properly ventilated to protect against premature roof
    deterioration and to promote a longer-lasting roof.


  5. Protective ridge-cap shingles
    We use a hip-and-ridge cap that is compatible with the roof shingles to guard
    against leakage at the ridge line and to enhance the appearance of your house or
    commercial building.
